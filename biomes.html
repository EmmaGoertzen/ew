Tundra Biome

Overview

  1. Avrage temperature is -34°c
  2. Worlds youngest biome
  3. Cover's about 20% of the earths surface
  4. Ground in permantly frozen
  5. Nights last for weeks, 24 hours of sunlight
  6. Land is barren
  7. Open treeless biome

Plants


  1. Artic Willow -
    Found in North American Tundra
    Grows Hair
    20cm in height
    Oval shaped leaves
  2. Diamond Leaf Willow -
    30ft tall
    Grows hairs
    looks like a bumpy, fuzzy carpet

Animals


  1. Musk Ox -
    Live in harsh conditions because of long hair and wooly undercoat
    Weight from 396-880lbs
    Only predator is the Artic wolf
    Eats plants to survive
  2. Artic Fox -
    Scavangers, Follow predators (such as polar bears) to eat their food remains/scraps
    10-16 Inches long and tend to weigh 6-12lbs
    Limbs are small to conserve heat
  3. Caribou -
    Short legs and brown coats that lighten in winter
    Large Hooves that support them over snow
    Good Swimmers
    Lower metabolic rate and go into semi-hibernation for bad wather
